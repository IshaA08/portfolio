About Me

Hi! I'm Isha - a web developer based out of Calgary, Alberta. The approach I have towards programming is to focus on creating clean, efficient and elegant solutions while continuously learning new industry-relevant skills.

More About Me

My passion for web development stems from my studies at the University of Calgary when completing the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science degree. I graduated from the program in November 2021. Since then, I have been working as a developer in the e-commerce domain.

While studying subjects such as full stack software development, data algorithms, data structures, operating systems, mathematics and statistics, I have completed multiple internships, capstone projects and programming hackathons. Generally, my main skills are in programming, software troubleshooting, testing, documentation, communication, organization, learning and teamwork.

With experience and achievements ranging from API development and automating business processes to managing multiple teams of volunteers at community events hosted by a non-profit organization, I am interested in applying my skills to collaboratively create high-quality code.
