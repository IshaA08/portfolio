About Me

Hi! I'm Isha - a web developer based out of Calgary, Alberta. The approach I have towards programming is to focus on creating clean, efficient and elegant solutions while continuously learning new industry-relevant skills. My passion for web development stems from my studies at the University of Calgary when completing the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program, which I graduated from in November 2021. With experience and achievements ranging from API development and automating business processes to managing multiple teams of volunteers at community events hosted by a non-profit organization, I am interested in applying my skills to collaboratively create high-quality code.
